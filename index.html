THE OUTING

An outburst of anger near the road

A refusal to speak on the path

A silence in the pine woods

A silence across the old railroad bridge

An attempt to be friendly in the water

A refusal to end the argument on the flat stones

A cry of anger on the steep bank of dirt

A weeping among the bushes
